Functionalities 
1. Students can create account using their unique reg no and other necessary details 
2. On registration, Students will verify face ID
3. Students will be able to register courses they are to sit for - before the examination 
4. Students can update certain details such as Level 
5. Students can update Face but will require approval from the Staff user

6. Staff can view all Students 
7. Staff can search Students by name to retrieve specific Student
8. Staff can scan for specific student using Face - this will be used to verify Students authenticity in examinations
9. Staff can Delete all Students of a class - in event of their graduation 
10. Staff can update every Students' details.
